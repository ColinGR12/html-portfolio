The Best Movies According to Colin

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

One Piece: Strong Worlds

This was one of the first anime movies I saw in a cinema when it came out.
The story was one of a kind and the animation was dope.

Your Name

A masterfully written story, involving time travel and romance it was a beautiful piece.

Spirited Away

Made by the classic Studio Ghibli this story is known by many. With an enticing story
and beautiful art work, it truly deserves the praise it gets.
